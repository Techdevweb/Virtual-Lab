Experiment 1


Experiment Name: DETERMINATION OF CELL MASS BY DIFFERENT METHODS

Theory

Growth can be defined in two ways either it is increase in cell number due to division of cells or increase in cell constituents. Microbial growth refers to the growth of total cell population due to cell divisions. The different methods for determination of biomass can be classified into 3 main categories –

1. General methods – Cell mass determination methods including dry weight, wet weight, Packed Cell Volume, Absorbance (optical density)

2. Method correlating increase in specific component like protein or nucleic acids with the growth

3. Methods correlating growth with metabolic activity like substrate consumption rate or product formation rate
